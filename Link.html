Introduction

Welcome to our Advocate Registration platform. Here, you can learn more about the registration process and how it works.

Advocate Registration Process

The advocate registration process involves the following steps:

  1. Fill out the registration form with your details.
  2. Submit the form for review.
  3. Our team will review your application and verify your information.
  4. Once approved, you will receive a confirmation email.
  5. You can then log in to your advocate account and start using our platform.
